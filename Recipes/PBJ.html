Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Simple sandwich with peanut butter and strawberry jelly

Ingredients

  • Two pieces of white bread
  • Jar of Strawberry Jam
  • Jar of Skippy's Peanut Butter

Instructions

  1. Get two slices of white bread
  2. Slather one slice with peanut butter
  3. Slather the other slice with jam
  4. Stack the two breads with the slathered sides facing themselves
  5. Enjoy!
