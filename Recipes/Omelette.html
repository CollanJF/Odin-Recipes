Breakfast Omelette

Description

Simple breakfast omelette perfect for any morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 slice of bacon
  • 1 Tomatoe
  • 1 scallion
  • Parsley

Cooking Instructions

  1. Dice the tomatoe
  2. Chop the scallion
  3. fry the bacon
  4. chop the bacon
  5. Scramble the raw eggs in a bowl
  6. Fry over medium heat with butter
  7. Before it gets solid, pour the bacon,scallions and tomatoe, on top of the egg
  8. Fold the omelette and turn down heat to low
  9. Flip omelette to prevent one side being overcooked
  10. Add parsley once finished
  11. Salt/pepper as required
