Mexican Shrimp Soup

Description

Classic seafood soup from the Mexican Pacific Coast

Rich in flavor, subtly spicy, and delicious

Ingredients

  • 1lb of shrimp
  • cabbage
  • carrots
  • Mexican squash
  • Russet potatoes
  • diced onions
  • tomato paste
  • serrano pepper
  • Can of Chipotle with adobo sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Chop all the veggies
  2. Peel all the shrimps
  3. Fry the onions with the tomatoe paste and chipotle
  4. Pour in all the chopped veggies
  5. Fill the pot with water about 3/4 of the way
  6. Bring to a boil; check if the carrots are thoroughly cooked
  7. Lastly pour in the shrimp; they cook fast so this must be last
  8. Cook until shrimps are cooked to liking
  9. Enjoy a bowl with lime and tortilla chips
